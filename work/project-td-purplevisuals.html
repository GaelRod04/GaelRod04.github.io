PURPLE VISUALS

A fully generative visual system built in TouchDesigner at its most raw — particle systems that listen to music and respond in real time, shifting and evolving with every beat, frequency and dynamic change in the audio.

The entire pipeline was designed with a narrative flow in mind: how the visuals enter, how they react, how they resolve. Conceived for a live concert or small installation context, where the music and the image become a single experience.
