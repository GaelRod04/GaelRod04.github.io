about

Visual artist working across motion graphics, 3D and real-time systems — building things that move, react and exist in spaces between art and technology.

I use Unreal Engine 5 to create renders, games and interactive environments that push what real-time can look and feel like. Alongside that, I develop custom 3D assets built for my own pipeline and aesthetic.

On the generative side, I design TouchDesigner systems — visuals, interactions, OSC networks and more — connecting software, hardware and space into live experiences.
